Tema 5: Past Simple

Acciones terminadas en un momento concreto del pasado.

Cheat: En negativa y pregunta se usa did y el verbo vuelve a base.

Formas principales

TipoAfirmativaNegativaPregunta
Regularworkeddid not workDid + sujeto + work?
Irregularwent / saw / haddid not go / see / haveDid + sujeto + go / see / have?
To bewas / werewas not / were notWas / Were + sujeto?

Usos del PDF

  • Accion terminada: I visited London last year.
  • Secuencia narrativa: She opened the door, sat down and smiled.
  • Habitos pasados: When I was a child, I played outside every day.
Marcadores: yesterday, last week, in 2020, two days ago.
