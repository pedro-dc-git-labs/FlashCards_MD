Bloque 7. There is / There are

Estructura para expresar existencia en singular y plural.

Singular y plural

FormaSingularPlural
AfirmativaThere is a book on the table.There are two books on the table.
NegativaThere is not a book on the table.There are not two books on the table.
PreguntaIs there a book on the table?Are there two books on the table?
