Adverbios de modo

Adverbs of Manner - Modifican al verbo y responden a ¿cómo?

Concepto básico

  • Normalmente modifican al verbo.
  • Responden a ¿cómo? / how?
  • Añaden información sobre la manera en la que se realiza una acción.
Regla general Ejemplos
Adjetivo + -ly quick → quickly · slow → slowly · careful → carefully
She ran quickly.  |  He answered politely.
Terminación del adjetivo Regla Ejemplo
-y -y → -ily happy → happily
-ic + -ally basic → basically
-le -le → -ly simple → simply
Irregulares (sin -ly) Adjetivo Adverbio Ejemplo (frase completa)
Forma igual fast fast He runs fast.
Forma igual hard hard She works hard every day.
Forma igual late late He arrived late.
Importante: no existen formas como fastly. Se dice fast.

Adverbios de modo más comunes

Adverbio Significado Ejemplo (frase completa)
quickly rápidamente She finished the test quickly.
slowly lentamente He walks slowly.
carefully cuidadosamente She checked the answer carefully.
easily fácilmente He passed the exam easily.
loudly ruidosamente They spoke loudly.
quietly silenciosamente She closed the door quietly.
badly mal He played badly.
well bien She sings well.
happily felizmente They lived happily together.
hard duro / mucho He works hard every day.
