Bloque 6. Past Continuous

Accion en progreso en un momento del pasado o como marco de otra accion.

Usage: was / were + verbo en -ing.

Usos principales

UsoEjemploMatiz
Accion en curso en un momento pasadoAt 8 p.m. I was studying.Proceso en desarrollo
Accion larga interrumpidaI was walking home when it started to rain.Marco + interrupcion
Dos acciones paralelasWhile she was cooking, I was setting the table.Simultaneidad
