Bloque 8. Verbos modales basicos

can, must, should y have to en usos elementales del nivel A1.

Modales del bloque

ModalValorEstructuraEjemplo
canCapacidad / permiso simplecan + verbo baseShe can swim.
mustObligacion fuertemust + verbo baseYou must wear a helmet.
shouldConsejoshould + verbo baseYou should rest.
have toObligacion externahave to + verbo baseI have to study.

FormaEjemploIdea
